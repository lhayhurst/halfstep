How to Play

Pick a scale from the top row, or leave it on Random. Choose a direction — ascending, descending, or both.

Play the notes using your MIDI keyboard or click the on-screen keys. Any octave counts — just get the right note name.

Correct notes flash green and advance. Wrong notes flash red and show you what was expected — play the right note to continue.

Streak counts consecutive perfect rounds. One mistake resets it.

Mastery

Each scale tracks your progress. Get 3 perfect rounds at each difficulty level to advance:

Silver — 3 clean rounds in Guided

Gold — 3 clean rounds in Shadow

Master — 3 clean rounds in Blind

Your progress is shown as a colored bar under each scale button.

Difficulty Modes

Guided — all scale notes are highlighted on the keyboard with labels. Great for learning new keys.

Shadow — only the root note is marked. You need to know the scale from memory.

Blind — the keyboard is hidden entirely. Pure ear and muscle memory.

Training Flow

Free Play is the default — the piano works as a regular instrument. Chords work too!

Hit Start to begin a drill. Complete the scale or hit Stop to bail out.

After each round, your results are logged in the Session Log at the bottom.

Tips

MIDI keyboards require Chrome or Edge. The on-screen keyboard works in any browser.

Use the MIDI Monitor at the bottom to debug your MIDI connection.
